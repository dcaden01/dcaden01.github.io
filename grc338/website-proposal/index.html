Introduction

Danny's Cookies

Danny's Cookies is a thriving bakery that primarily thrives on the fact that is open 24hrs. So if you stay out late with friends its no matter. The bakery sells sweet and savory cookies, that fill you up to your hearts content.

Target audience

The primary target audience will be late teens to young adults. People who stay out late with friends after going out. They will mostly likely be our primary customers due to pretty open hours.

Their primary tasks will be to find out what the menu is. Creating outreach introducing to people what Danny's cookies are. The second tasks will be directing them to the store or to call for orders. Once they realize that we will open 24hrs it will help with business.
